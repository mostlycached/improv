Usage Instructions

  1. Navigate to any webpage you want to create an ad for
  2. Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar
  3. Click "Generate Ad" to create an AI-powered advertisement
  4. Download, edit, or share your generated ad

The extension will automatically extract content from the current webpage and generate relevant ad copy and visuals using your configured backend service.
